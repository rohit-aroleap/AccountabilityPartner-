## Summary
- Dashboard ideas: 100 across 12 categories
- Prompt engineering ideas: 98 across 14 categories
- Top emergent themes:
  1. **Context data is richer than what the prompt sees** — notes field, onboarding answers (age, goal), exercise names from the Ferra export are all stored but not injected into prompts
  2. **Quality feedback loop is incomplete** — ratings are stored in Firebase (`aiRatings`) but never surfaced in the UI; the feedback can't drive prompt tuning without a way to review it
  3. **Operator cognitive load at scale** — with 10+ customers, the single-chat-at-a-time mental model breaks down; multi-customer ops, drafts queue, and attention signals are the most impactful near-term work
