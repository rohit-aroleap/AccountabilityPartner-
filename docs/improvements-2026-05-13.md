## Summary
- Dashboard ideas: 94 across 12 categories
- Prompt engineering ideas: 96 across 14 categories
- Top emergent themes:
  1. **Context quality gap** — The AI sees a flat list of 20 messages but lacks structured signals (notes, stated goals, commitment history, exercise names) that would make every draft significantly more specific.
  2. **Ops blindspots at scale** — The dashboard works well for 5-10 customers but will break on 20+: no search, no sort, no batch actions, no cross-customer activity feed.
  3. **Prompt drift is invisible** — There's no systematic way to detect that a prompt change degraded quality across even a small golden test set; the rating system captures anecdotes but not trends.
